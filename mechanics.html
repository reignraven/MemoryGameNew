How to play...

The Memory game is a single-player game which you need to match the pair of letters. First, try opening random cards, then when you saw two matching cards, click the 1st cards then its pair. If you choose the wrong pair, the cards will flip to close again. Just keep on repeating the steps until all the pairs are matched.

This module is designed for those who love to train visual memory as well as for games like bingo and cards. The objective of such games would be memorizing of the location of the cards or designating the correct matches.

The mechanic evolved from a classic board game Memory, where two of more people should find identical pairs of cards.
