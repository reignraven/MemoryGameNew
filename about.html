The Creators...

This game was created as one of the requirements in the subject "Wireless Application" of the students of St. Benilde Center for Global Competence Inc. under the Bachelor of Science in Information Technology program.

The group "MONOCHROMATECH" created this simple game application as a Pre-Final project. The group is consists of four members, two programmers, an analyst and a designer.

Mr. Ian Benedict D. Cabodoc, the leader and the main programmer of the group was the one researched and programmed the main game, including its scoring system. The Analyst, Ms. Mary Joanne B. Olino compile all the assests, files and parts of the application and also manage how the system will run. Ms. Jessica Joyce Q. Manalang, the designer choose the color theme for the game and created the cover page of the application. And lastly, the assistant programmer, Ms. Mary Grace Porneso help in developing the game system.
